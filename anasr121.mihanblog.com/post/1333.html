Justice - انصاف و عدالت

نویسنده :ع.ا نصر
تاریخ:دوشنبه سیزدهم آبان 1392-08:53 ق.ظ


The WORLD is facing a GREAT REVOLUTION, a  demand for  JUSTICE ; the similar slogan of uprised nations.

    35 years ago(1978), the Islamic revolution appeared in Iran was barely believed by politicians. What happened was due to pure Islamic concepts described by Imam Khomeini in his honest and faithfull words. Many, anticipated this Islamic revolution a transitory  movement. It could not only survive, but grow up contrary to unlimited menaces from all superpowers. This was in a great conflict with which west has been trying to stablish all over the world. The prominent point that nations who lived dependent and united to west powers,  are now observing day to day significant progresses of this young Islamic power. The political, economical and industrial achievements in Iran is attracting looks and they think about their country's conditions, in comparison. What have they obtain after along period of saying YES to WEST?

   To answer, people protest against the governing policies. Protesters demand end to rule of monarch regimes. They uprise for justice and freedom. These nations have seen the authority,    workers who obbey US and Israel dictated orders to provide their benefits. All these years the rulers have been playing opposite to Iran, so the people percieve all of their lags and poverty the results of US friendship. Tunisia, Egypt, Libya and other countries in mideast are following this model. They want end to paying for US weaponary help and Israel inteferes.       

    US and its allies are concerned about new situation in the Mideast, additional to previous imbroglio of Iraq and Afghanistan. Immunity of Israel and energy resources are two taboos for west and these uprisings are threatening both. Deceitful behavior of superpowers is obviously visible in the new scene. US firstly attempts to protect  these obedient regimes. If  awakening protests despaires, US changes the role in appearance and shows a democratic face to keep influence in these regions. For this, they will try to penetrate into people streams and make up for a new faced ruler on top but retain same body of authority.  This is done to bring back the old allied goverments by causing deviation into revolutions. They will use all capabilities such as disinformation, hidden financial support and unifying everyone who shows pro-west trends.

    To remain silent and UN Security Council feeble reactions are evidence to west policies. Blockading billion dollars of monarch wealth in western banks doesn't seem sufficient, as they have blockaded much wealth which Iran's "Shaah" had sent to those countries. Eeven what was concluded in official contracts with US has never been sent back to Iran. These dollars are people's properties and US and allies despite claiming human rights and democracy, have dishonestly struggled against Iranian people rights.  Only unanimous and resisting leadership, cautious about all possible threats, can afford the people the chance to persue their willings until establishing desired governments.
